Edit this template to customise the "account activation" HTML email your users will receive. If you are adding URLs into this, remember that only absolute URLS (with a domain name) make sense in email! They can be served from any stable location, including your Galaxy server or GitHub. The following variables are available for inserting into the html with Jinja2 syntax, like {{ variable_name }}.
